Frequently Asked Questions

Just WHY?

Why 17 LCC per LTC?

We like this number ;)

Is this safe?

Absolutely. Your private keys stay safe with you, we don't ask them. We can calculate your "mirror" LCC address without your private keys, just from your public LTC address.

Can I send LTC from exchange?

NO! This address belongs to exchange, and exchange can't (in general) deposit returned LTC back to your account. Also, we can't guarantee that exchange will deposit free LCC to your account too.

Can I send LTC directly from Ledger/Trezor/... hardware wallet?

Sure.

Can I send LTC from SegWit-address (starting 3... or M...)?

Yes. Your LTC will be returned to same address. Your free LCC will be sent to LCC SegWit-address too.

What if I send LTC after distribution ends?

You will receive all your LTC back, but your will not receive free LCC.

What if I make several transactions from same LTC address?

All your LTC will be returned to you, but your will receive free LCC only for your first transaction (17X amount of first transaction).

Can I participate if already participated in Fork?

Everyone can participate. No matter participated you (your LTC address) in Fork or not.
